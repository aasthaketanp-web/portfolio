About

Clarity is
the whole point

I care about data that's accurate, accessible, and actually trusted by the people who need it.

I'm a Data Engineer based in Indianapolis. At RISE INDY, I own the organization's data platform — making architectural calls, building pipelines, and designing the dashboards that stakeholders use to understand student impact.

My work lives at the intersection of infrastructure and intention. I use Microsoft Fabric, PySpark, and SQL to build Lakehouse architectures that don't just process data quickly — they make data trustworthy. Deduplication, schema validation, consistent definitions: the unsexy work that makes everything else reliable.

I hold an M.S. in Data Science from Indiana University Bloomington and a B.E. in Information Technology. Before RISE INDY, I worked as a Data Analyst at the Indiana Department of Revenue and interned across three organizations, learning data engineering from the ground up.

My instinct is to understand the problem before proposing the solution — which means I ask a lot of questions, think carefully about framing, and build things that hold up over time.
